About me

I am a PhD student @COSIC under the supervision of Prof. Bart Preneel. My research interests include cryptography and coding theory. I have been working on the following topics:

  • Symmetric Key Cryptanalysis: Stream Ciphers and Block Ciphers
  • Design of Symmetric Key Primitives
  • Computing over Encrypted Data
